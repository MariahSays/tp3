15 Minute Healthy Cashew Chicken

Ingredients

Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese black vinegar
  • 1.5 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Pinch white pepper
Cashew Chicken
  • 1 cup Raw cashews
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into 1-inch cubes Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bunch green onion - 1” segments
  • 4 cloves garlic, rough chop
  • 1-inch knob ginger, grated
  • 1 rib celery, medium dice
  • 1 green bell pepper, ½” dice
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts, halved or quartered

Kitchen Equipment

  • whisk or fork
  • 13" x 9" baking pan
  • Wok
  • Measuring cups
  • Measuring spoons
  • Spatula
  • Knife

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Place butter in 13" x 9" inch pan and melt in oven. Swirl to coat bottom and sides with butter.
  3. Spread graham cracker crumbs evenly over bottom of pan.
  4. Layer chocolate chips, butterscotch chips, and nuts over crumbs.
  5. Pour condensed milk over nuts.
  6. Sprinkle coconut over condensed milk.
  7. Bake until edges are golden brown, about 25 minutes.
  8. Let cool before cutting into bars.
